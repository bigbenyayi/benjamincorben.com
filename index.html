My Motivation

Creating


When facing an empty Android Studio project, a flow of possibilities and ideas go through my mind. That's when I realize, I can create anything I want, from nothing.

Innovating


I love change. Development is all about changes. Changing the User Interface, enhancing the code files, updating new features. Constantly trying to improve.

Money


I did not choose the Android Development route for the money though I find the business side very appealing. I have many projects that I would love to get underway. Some of them require some initial fundings; this is where the money gets interesting for me.

Going big


The 2 billion monthly active Android devices open up so many different options as a developer. I could be part of one of the biggest companies in the world, working on exciting projects to change the lives. I could also start my own projects and reserve a corner of this huge market all to myself.
